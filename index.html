Why import cars from the USA?

We compared the US market with the European one and realized that buying a car in America is much more profitable, as paradoxical as it may sound. This is due to well-thought-out logistics, advanced vehicle assessment services, and the overall car purchasing process.

Most US citizens lease a car for several years, and during this time the leasing company takes care of all regular maintenance. As a result, cars from the USA are one of the best options for car enthusiasts in France.

Why such a low price?

Damaged cars from the USA are purchased from insurance company auctions. At these auctions, a car loses up to half of its value even with minimal damage. Considering the costs of purchase, shipping, customs clearance, and repair, the price of a similar car in France is 35–50% higher, while new cars are astronomically expensive.
